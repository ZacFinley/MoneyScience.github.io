Obviously we cannot just pick our growth percentage.

If someone is tryig to sell you an investment with a guaranteed growth/interest rate be careful.
Savings accounts, bonds and CD's will have a starting interest rate but that can change over the life of the account depending on the speficic terms.
Any stock cannot be guaranteed.
Annuities may include a guarantee for a set amount of years, this is costing you upfront so check the fine print.
