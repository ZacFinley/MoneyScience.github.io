Net Worth History

You have been tracking your progress all year.
Now you want to see a graph of all your hard work.
Enter in all the balances from your accounts for each month of the year.
You can then see how they grew and how they affected your Net Worth.
As you enter the amounts will see two prediction lines.
One is showing you how the account might look if, based on the entered information, the average dollar amount was to be applied to the remaining months.
The other is showing the average percentage was applied to the remaining months.
